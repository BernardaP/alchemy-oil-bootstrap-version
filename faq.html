FAQ

What forms of payment do you accept?

We accept all major cards: credit, debit and gift cards, i.e., Visa, Mastercard, Discover and American Express. Additionally, we accept Apple Pay and PayPal.

Can you provide a discount code for my order?

You will receive a discount code for $3.00 off your purchase via email when you return an empty oil bottle and tube to Alchemy Oil using the prepaid return label..

All bottles, tubes, paper and mailers are recyclable. Please continue to help us be as sustainable as possible by recycling your bottle, packaging tube, paper and shipping box. Please save the tube your oil arrived in as well as the oil bottle. When you mail your used products back, you not only make Mother Nature smile, but you will receive $3.00 off your next purchase.

Please ensure you rinse your oil bottle out prior to packing it or screw the dropper on securely to ensure the oil does not leak.

How can I receive free shipping on my order?

Buy two or more bottles of Alchemy Oil and the shipping is on us!

What is the difference in the two products sold on AlchemyOil.com?

One mixture contains lavender and the other does not. During our testing process, we discovered a lot of women are allergic to lavender, so we offer a blend of Alchemy Oil without lavender.

Do you offer additional size bottles of oil?

At this time Alchemy Oil only sells 4 oz. bottles of our mixture. We are giving thought to an 8 oz. bottle in the future.

What if I had an allergic reaction to the oil?

We recommend you discontinue use immediately and contact your physician. They can provide you details on what ingredient you may have had a reaction to. All ingredients are listed on the Shop page of the website, so please read that thoroughly before you buy.

Where can I read a full list of ingredients?

Please go to our Oil page where you will find the full list.

Does your hair oil work for all hair of color including biracial or mixed hair?

Do I still need moisturizer when using your oil?

Alchemy has replaced my moisturizers; the only other products I use are shampoo, leave in conditioner, curl definer and water! Oil seals in moisture with the use of water. This is why hair should be wet when applying oil during your wash process before braiding.

If you have diagnosed scalp or dermatology issues that result in excessive dryness, please consult your doctor for additional moisturizer solutions.

When I apply Alchemy 2-3 times a week, should I be applying anything else with it?

This answer is based on wearing a fully natural style (non-straightened hair), if you have locs, wear braid outs, twist outs etc... To oil scalp, wet hands and edges and apply the oil using the dropper onto scalp. Massage scalp for about 3-5 minutes.

If you wear a wash and go, spritz hair with a water bottle and use the dropper to disperse oil onto scalp. Massage scalp for about 3-5 minutes.

Can you recommend a shampoo, conditioner, curl definer that would work best with your oil?

We encourage women to use shampoos, conditioners and other hair products that are sulfate and paraben free, contain no glycol, phthalates, petroleum, have natural and minimal ingredients and water.

How is your oil different from other oils out there?

Alchemy Oil is 100% natural and only contains 11 or 10 basic ingredients (version without lavender). We sell a product that encourages hair growth but also promote a healthy lifestyle that will further support hair strength, growth and health.

How can I request to be a guest on the Hair to Toe blog?

Please email us at info@alchemy-oil.com and title your email “Hair to Toe Guest Blog.” Provide your full name and a description of what you do and what you’d like to highlight on the blog. We ask that you keep submissions to about 1,000 words in length (can be shorter) and provide any social media handles and a valid phone number. Be patient with us after you submit your entry. Submissions take about two weeks to read through. We will notify you by email if you are selected. Your post will go up one week after you are emailed.

Do you welcome ideas/ thoughts for blog topics?

Why was my comment not added to the comment section on the website?
