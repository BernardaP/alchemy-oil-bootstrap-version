Hair Journey

This is a place to highlight and celebrate all hair. Our hair is so unique, we can do so much to it and with it! Maybe you recently worked up the courage to do the big chop, you stopped using relaxers and perms, you took your weave out, you are on a beautiful loc journey or you are growing your hair back. Whatever your story, we want to hear it, see it and share it with other women who might be on the same journey or struggling with the same concerns.

Disclaimer

Hey, love, please keep hair journey submissions to a few paragraphs in length. Please include your first name (last name is not required) and be patient with us after you submit your entry. Submissions take about two weeks to read through and an additional week to post.

For your photo, give us a headshot, confident selfie or a pic you would send to your crush! Ensure your photo is as clear and crisp as possible. We want to capture you and your crown in all its glory. Thank you!
